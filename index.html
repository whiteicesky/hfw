THE MOST FAMOUS CREATORS

Rick Owens

The monochrome color scheme, the architectural cut with beveled seams, the asymmetrical details, and the elongated sleeves are all signature techniques of Rick Owens' creative direction, which have made his brand known and loved around the world.

Balenciaga

High fashion is like an orchestra, with Balenciaga as the conductor. The other couturiers, are like musicians, following his lead.

Vetements

The genius of Vetements lies in the way they use and adapt popular culture, creating witty and attractive things.

Prada

An Italian fashion house that specializes in the creation of men's and women's clothing, as well as shoes, accessories, and perfumes.
